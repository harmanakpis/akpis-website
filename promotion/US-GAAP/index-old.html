FAQ's

Will I be able to travel the world and make a living simultaneously?
Indeed, as the certification of Certified Management Accountant USA is a globally recognised one, one can make the most of the opportunity and work from any place on this globe.
Am I eligible to do the CMA USA course after my 12th?
Of course, one can start the preparation for the CMA course after completing their 12th. One is eligible to take the exam for the same if they have passed the 12th standard from any stream. So, against the common belief, one is not expected to get a PG degree.
What will be my job profile after I get my CMA USA certification?
  • Cost accountants
  • Financial Controller
  • Financial Analyst
How many papers am I required to clear to get my CMA USA certification?
The CMA part is divided into 2 parts.

Part one has 100 MCQs and 2 essay questions. With the following sections:

  • Section A: External Financial Reporting Decisions
  • Section B: Planning, Budgeting and Forecasting
  • Section C: Performance Management
  • Section D: Cost Management
  • Section E: Internal Control
  • Section F: Technology and Analytics
One is expected to score at least 360 marks out of 500 to clear the exam.

Part two has 100 MCQs and 2 essay questions. With the following sections:

  • Section A: Financial Statement Analytics
  • Section B: Planning, Budgeting and Forecasting
  • Section C: Performance Management
  • Section D: Cost Management
  • Section E: Internal Control
  • Section F: Technology and Analytics
Part two is comparatively easier than the first part and one can score almost 90% with the right guidance from AKPIS Professionals.
